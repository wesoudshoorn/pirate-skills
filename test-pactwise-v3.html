FAQ

Common questions

Are the e-signatures legally binding?

Yes. Pactwise e-signatures comply with ESIGN, UETA, and eIDAS regulations. Every signature includes a full audit trail with timestamps, IP addresses, and browser fingerprints.

Can I use my own contract templates?

Absolutely. Upload any Word or PDF template and Pactwise will convert it into an editable, signable format. You can also save custom clauses to your library.

What payment methods do clients see?

Through our Stripe integration, clients can pay via credit card, debit card, ACH bank transfer, or Apple Pay. Funds land in your account within 2 business days.

Is there a contract limit on the free plan?

The Starter plan lets you manage up to 3 active contracts at a time, with no time limit. Once a contract is completed or archived, you can create a new one.
