FAQ

Common questions

Does CalmDesk actually block notifications or just hide them?

It batches them. Nothing is lost. During a focus window, notifications queue silently and arrive as a single digest when the window ends. Urgent messages (defined by your team) still break through instantly.

What if someone on my team doesn't want to participate?

CalmDesk works at the individual level too. But the real power is team-wide adoption. Most teams see holdouts come around within a week once they see the focus analytics and feel the difference in async communication quality.

How long does setup take?

Under 10 minutes. Connect Slack and your calendar, set your first focus window, and you're live. Team rollout usually takes a day — we provide a Slack message template your team lead can send.

Can I try it before committing my team?

Yes. The Solo plan is free forever. Use it personally for a week, then upgrade to Team when you're ready to roll it out. No credit card required to start.
