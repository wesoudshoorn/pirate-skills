BUILT FOR API-FIRST TEAMS

Every endpoint, every deploy, every anomaly

Driftwood isn't another uptime checker. It understands your API's behavior over time and knows when something is off.

Endpoint-level health scores

Each endpoint gets a health score from 0-100, updated every 30 seconds. Combines latency p50/p95/p99, error rate, and response body validation into a single number your team can act on.

Deploy-aware baselines

Driftwood detects deploys automatically. Baselines reset, so a new slow endpoint doesn't get lost in historical averages.
