How it works

Smart Focus Scheduling

CalmDesk reads your calendar, finds the best windows for deep work, and blocks them automatically. It learns your energy patterns and schedules focus when you're most productive.

Distraction Blocking

Silences Slack, mutes notifications, and blocks time-wasting sites during focus sessions. One click to start, zero discipline needed.
