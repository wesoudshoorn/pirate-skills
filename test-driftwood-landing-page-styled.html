Built for API-first teams

Every endpoint, every deploy, every anomaly

Driftwood isn't another uptime checker. It understands your API's behavior over time and knows when something is off.

Endpoint-level health scores

Each endpoint gets a health score from 0-100, updated every 30 seconds. Combines latency p50/p95/p99, error rate, and response body validation into a single number your team can act on.

Deploy-aware baselines

Driftwood detects deploys automatically. Baselines reset, so a new slow endpoint doesn't get lost in historical averages.

Root cause in 30 seconds

Every alert includes: the deploy that caused it, the exact endpoint, a diff of response behavior, and the engineer who shipped the change.

Slack, PagerDuty, and webhook alerts

Alerts go where your team already works. Smart grouping prevents alert fatigue: related failures are bundled into a single incident, not 47 separate pings.

OpenAPI schema validation

Upload your OpenAPI spec. Driftwood validates every response against it. When a field changes type or a required field disappears, you know immediately, not when a frontend engineer files a bug.
